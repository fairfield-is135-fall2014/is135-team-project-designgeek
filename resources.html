Roadmap

White the precise timing and mix of events varies from year to year -- see the front page of the website for details -- the calendar generally goes something like this:

  • September 23rd(Tuesday): Kickoff (begin student signups, introduce mentors, ...)
  • October 19th(Sunday): Startup Day (concept pitches, workshop with mentors, team signups)
  • November 15th(Saturday): Elevator Pitch (icebreaker/mixer, prizes
  • January 27th(Tuesday): Spring Kickstart
  • February 2nd Team Formation Deadline
  • Feb (Dates TBA): Open Office Hours with Expert Advisors - Appts. Mandatory (Informational events with subject matter experts)
  • February 19 and 24th: (Thursday/Tuesday): Practice Pitch Sessions with Mentors (must have preliminary draft plan)
  • February 28th (Sunday): Semifinal Round (must have complete plan, though revisions can be made before final round
  • March 15th Deadline for final plan revisions
  • March 31 (Tuesday): Finale - Cash prizes to be announced at the Gala Event

The best teams have a plan for each event, working to get the maximum benefit from the business connections, mentoring, funding, and other resources available at the time
