Startup Day:


Startup Day is open to any student interested in working on a business plan who might want to join the 2015 Business Plan Competition. We encourage those who have the business idea and those who have specific business skills to come! Attendees will:


  • Have an opportunity to pitch ideas to a live audience
  • Form teams drawing on the skills of the people they meet
  • Brainstrom with successful entrepreneur mentors on building your "business"

Mentors will coach teams through business modeling in preparation for the Elevator Pitch Competition in Novemeber where there is a $2,500 in prize money to be awarded!

Elevator Pitch Contest


Come get your "Shark Tank" presentation skills mastered at the 2015 BPC Elevator Pitch Contest! Didd we mention that there is a $2,500 in prize money to be awarded to the teams that best convinceour judges to invest in their busiens? This interactive event is for all students interested in presenting better. Some teams are focused on learning how to pitch their business ideas and others will come to learn how to present an effective pitch. Believe it or not, this skill will come in handy throughout your life! We promise you won't regret spending a few hours with the BPC team!


The day will go as follows:

  • Dr. Mousumi Bose Godbole explains the basics of creating an elevator pitch.
  • Students try their hands at 30-second product pitches.
  • Mentors deliver a presentation on business plan fundamentals, the role of elevator pitches, etc.
  • Lunch/Breakout sessions: Teams work on elevator pitches for a business plan.
  • Students break up into teams of and either prepare their personal or an assigned business pitch.
  • Teams will pitch to a group of judges and winners will be selected.
